02 / 03 · Fraud Anatomy
How Every
Scam Works
The 3-step pattern behind 89% of Security Guard fraud
1
The Fake Job Post
Real company name + inflated salary + urgency. Names like "RD Security Services" blend in among thousands of real Delhi NCR firms. Impossible to distinguish without a database check.
2
The WhatsApp Redirect
Contact moves off-platform immediately. "Call me on WhatsApp for a quick interview." Once off-platform, Job Hai loses all visibility, and so does the job seeker.
3
The Registration Fee
₹500–₹3,000 for a "uniform deposit" or "ID verification." The ask feels legitimate — security jobs do require uniforms. Victim pays. Recruiter disappears.
₹1,500
Average amount lost per victim per incident in Delhi NCR Security Guard scams — equivalent to 3–4 days of earnings for the target demographic.

3 design interventions to eliminate Security Guard fraud
01
Pre-Post Verification
AI flags recruiter before listing goes live. Company name + GST + phone cross-checked against blacklist in real time.
↓ 68% fraud
02
Exit Intent Shield
Detects and blocks WhatsApp redirect patterns in job descriptions. Keeps contact within the platform.
↓ 41% complaints
03
1-Tap Fraud Report
Reduces reporting from 7 screens to a single post-call tap. Surfaces to job seekers during and immediately after calls.
